Bulgogi recipe

How to Make Beef Bulgogi

What is Bulgogi?

Korean beef barbecue or Bulgogi (literally "fire meat") is made of thin slices of ribeye, tenderloin, or sirloin marinated in a savory-sweet sauce, grilled on a barbecue or on a stove-top griddle.

Beef Bulgogi ingredients

Use the following ingredients for your amazing Beef Bulgogi:

  • Beef Steak

  • Rice

  • Broccoli

  • Green Onion

  • Green Pepper

You can use Bulgogi Korean BBQ sauce or make your own marinade from the following ingredients:

  • Soy Sauce

  • Sesame Oil

  • Sesame Seeds

  • Sugar

  • Pepper

Steps

To make your Beef Bulgogi, follow these two simple steps:

  1. Marinate the Steak

    Place the steak in a shallow bowl. In a separate bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients and whisk until blended. Pour the marinade over the steak and make sure the meat is completely covered. Allow the beef to marinate in the fridge for about an hour or you can leave it overnight for the best taste.

  2. Grill the Steak

    Cook the beef on a lightly oiled grill set to high heat until it's completely cooked through. This should take about 1 or 2 minutes on each side. Feel free to serve your bulgogi with rice and kimchi. Add some steamed veggies on the side to make it well - balanced.
